WHO ARE WE ?

PWAs take advantage of the latest technologies to combine the best of web and mobile apps. This podcast looks into recent advancements in the P.W.A technologies and the opportunities we, as developers,and bussinesses have to use this new generation of applications.

Progressive web apps could be the next big thing for the mobile web. Originally proposed by Google in 2015, they have attracted a lot of attention because of their ease of development. Think of P.W.As as a websites built using web technologies but act and feel like an native apps.
